Welcome to Sree Muruga Swamy Temple

The world-famous Vayaranmaruthi Sree Murugaswamy Temple is located in the Perunad Panchayat of Pathanamthitta District. On the Pathanamthitta-Sabarimala road, you can reach the Temple, which is located at the top of the hill, by climbing 408 steps from the Vayaranmaruthi Swamipady junction.This is a rare temple where the sun shines on the Muruka idol in the sanctum sanctorum of the temple facing east read more...

MURUGAN WORSHIP

Every day the temple opens at 5 am and after one o'clock afternoon poojas the temple closed. Again visit the temple from 5 pm to 8 pm. On Sundays, Tuesdays, Thursdays and Fridays, Bahmashree Saju Swamy performs prophecies in the presence of Lord Murugan in front of the temple from 8 am to 1 pm.

IMPORTANCE OF SHASTI

Lord Muruga is that energy which helps us in attaining the moksha. That energy which helps unite the Kundalini Sakthi with Shiva, the higher consciousness, the child uniting the parents.

UPA PRATHISHTA

Though the main prathishta (idol) is Load Sree Murugaswamy, there are also upa devatas: Yogeeswaran, Malanada, Hidumban, Sree Durgadevi, Ganapathi, Nagarajavu are the Upa devatas. Each of them has separate temple abodes and daily poojas.
