About the Temple

The world-famous Vayaranmaruthi Sree Murugaswamy Temple is located in the Perunad Panchayat of Pathanamthitta District. On the Pathanamthitta-Sabarimala road, you can reach the Temple, which is located at the top of the hill, by climbing 408 steps from the Vayaranmaruthi Swamipady junction.

This is a rare temple where the sun shines on the Muruka idol in the sanctum sanctorum of the temple facing east.

The origin stories of the temple are also very special, Years ago, at 12 o'clock on the night of Thiruvonam, a boy named Saju, who was only fifteen years old, got a vision of Murugan. The boy, who was facing death, was told, "All your ailments will be cured and I will stay here for the salvation of the pious." The next day the boy's illness was completely cured. The story spread in the local place. Then people from various castes came there and prayed to Lord Murugan.

Rituals and rituals have many features. Muruga's vision also has the power to cure disability, infertility, healing, academic success and hearing loss.
